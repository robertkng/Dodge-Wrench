“If you can dodge a wrench, you can dodge a ball.”

TUTORIAL SECTION

Game Rules:

A wrench is released and ricochets off the wall at random
points within the confines of the gameboard.
There is only one rule, maneuver your player around to
avoid the wrench.
The game ends once the ball makes contact with you.

How to control your player:

Press the left arrow key to move your player to the left.
Press the up arrow key to move your player upwards.
Press the right arrow key to move your player to the right.
Press the down arrow key to move your player downwards.
